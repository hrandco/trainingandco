FAQs

What kinds of services do Training and Co. provide?

Training and Co. offers a range of professional and personal development training services designed to meet your unique business needs:

1. Facilitated Expert-Led Training Sessions
Collaborative and interactive training sessions tailored to your business.

These can be conducted:

  • Onsite at your workplace
  • Offsite at a venue of your choice
  • Virtually via an online platform

Our experienced trainers bring real-world HR and business expertise, ensuring sessions are practical, engaging, and impactful.

2. Online Workplace Learning
Customised learning through a branded Learning Management System (LMS), including:

  • A library of 400+ courses across various industries, updated with legislative changes by Australian employment lawyers.
  • Integration with your existing LMS, or we can set up a new one for your business.
  • Conversion of your internal training materials into professional online courses.
  • The flexibility to add more courses to your tailored library as your business grows and evolves, ensuring your team’s learning stays relevant and aligned with your goals.


3. Online Courses on Our Website

  • Access our 400+ online courses directly through our website. These courses are available to the public on a pay-per-course basis, making them accessible to individuals and businesses alike.

Contact us to discover how we can support your employees’ professional development or your own personal growth.

Who is HR and Co. Consulting?

HR and Co. Consulting is our subsidiary business, providing comprehensive HR solutions for small to medium-sized businesses, including:

  • Employment Relations: Workforce planning, employment contracts, Award interpretation, recruitment, onboarding, performance management, and more.
  • Workplace Health and Safety (WHS): Policies, WHS audits, site inspections, and Workers’ Compensation support.
  • HR Systems and Audits: HR audits, policies, employee handbooks, remuneration checks, and tailored HRIS implementation.
  • Specialised Support: Psychometric assessments, workplace investigations, and other expert services.

Learn more at www.hrandco.com.au or Contact Us to discuss your HR needs.

Do I need to enter a contract to work with Training and Co.?

No, we don’t require lock-in contracts. For LMS subscriptions, we recommend maintaining the service for at least 12 months to fully benefit from professional online courses for your team members. You can cancel in line with the agreed terms.

Can I book training if I’m not a client?

Absolutely! You don’t need to be an existing client or have a contract to book facilitated training sessions. We’ll invoice you based on the services provided.
