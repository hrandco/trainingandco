Case Study: Simplifying HR for a Growing Roofing Company

Company Snapshot

A successful roofing company with 25 employees experienced rapid growth over the past two years. With high standards and a clear growth strategy, the company is expanding its team and services but is struggling to balance HR administration with focusing on the business.

The Challenge

As the company grew, outdated employment contracts and time-consuming HR processes became a burden. They needed employment contracts tailored to casual, full-time, part-time wages and salaried employees, along with streamlined HR policies and onboarding documentation. The business owners wanted to reduce administrative tasks, ensure legislative compliance, and focus on scaling their operations and services.

Case Study: Our Solutions

Solution 1: Employment Contracts

We provided updated, legislatively compliant contract templates for casual, full-time, part-time wages and salaried employees. These templates were easy to use, ensuring clarity and compliance across the business and its team.

Solution 2: Onboarding Documentation

We created a professional online onboarding process with customised templates and updated forms. This new process improved the company's recruitment experience, leaving a positive impression on new recruits and elevating their employer brand.

Solution 3: HR Policies

We created tailored HR policies aligned with the company’s growth strategy. These policies ensured compliance and supported employee satisfaction and retention by creating a structured and fair workplace environment.

Solution 4: HR Information System (HRIS)

To streamline HR further, we recommended implementing an HRIS within six months. This system will centralise employee records, automate routine tasks, improve compliance, and provide valuable data-driven insights for decision-making.

Case Study: The Results

The company quickly experienced the benefits of a simplified and professional HR framework:

  • Reduced Administration: Time spent on HR tasks decreased significantly with streamlined contracts and processes in place.
  • Boosted Employer Brand: A polished onboarding process enhanced their image and helped attract top talent.
  • Improved Employee Retention: Clear, consistent contracts and tailored policies increased employee satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Prepared for Growth: The planned implementation of an HRIS will further enhance efficiency, helping the company manage its expanding workforce efficiently.

With a strong HR foundation, the owners can now focus on scaling their business; confident, compliant and efficient systems support their workforce.

What kinds of services do you provide?

HR and Co. Consulting offers comprehensive advice and support on all aspects of employee management for your business. Our services include:


  • Employment Relations: Workforce planning, employment contracts, Award interpretation, employee classifications and pay rates, recruitment, onboarding, induction, performance management, and change management.
  • Workplace Health and Safety (WHS): Policies and procedures, WHS audits, site inspections, and Worker's Compensation claim coordination and support.
  • HR Systems and Audits: HR audits, policies and procedures, employee handbooks, remuneration checks, and implementing Human Resource Information Systems (HRIS) tailored to your business needs.
  • Specialised Support: Psychometric assessments, workplace investigations, and more.

We’re here to help you navigate compliance requirements and implement practical solutions to enhance your workplace.
